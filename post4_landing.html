Who We Are

Post 4 Investigations Unit

The Post 4 Investigations Unit serves as the detective division of Kentucky State Police Post 4, under the command of Investigations Lieutenant Levi Scott. The unit investigates Group A offenses across an eight-county region in central and western Kentucky.

Detectives assigned to Post 4 are responsible for conducting thorough, professional investigations into serious criminal activity, from the initial response through case prosecution, with a focus on serving and protecting the communities within their jurisdiction.

Members of the Kentucky State Police Investigations Unit operate under a clear standard of duty and conduct. The role demands service to the public through the protection of life and property, the defense of the innocent, and the preservation of constitutional rights grounded in liberty, equality, and justice.

Detectives are expected to maintain integrity in both personal and professional conduct, exercise sound judgment under pressure, and remain consistently aware of the impact of their actions on others. Confidential information obtained in the course of duty is safeguarded and disclosed only when necessary to fulfill official responsibilities.

All enforcement actions are carried out without bias or personal influence. The law is applied firmly and fairly, without fear, favoritism, or unnecessary force. The position carries a responsibility to uphold public trust, with the badge representing a commitment to ethical service and accountability.
